How to play

  • Swipe in any direction to move your snake.
  • Follow the arrows to find the correct path.
  • Avoid traps, walls, and dead-ends.
  • Collect coins and unlock new snake skins.
  • Reach the exit to escape each maze.

Frequently asked questions

My progress is gone after reinstalling the app.

Progress is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app erases it. We are working on optional cloud save in a future update.

I'm stuck on a level.

Use the hint button in the top bar. You can earn extra hints by watching a short ad. You can also replay earlier levels to practice.

Are there in-app purchases?

No. Snake Escape Puzzle is supported by ads only — no IAP, no accounts, no subscriptions.

The game lags on my older device.

Try closing other apps and restarting your device. If it persists, please email us with your device model and iOS version.

Will more levels be added?

Yes! We regularly add new level packs through updates. Make sure you're on the latest version from the App Store.
